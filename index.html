Welcome to the Sleep Awareness Project

Helping students improve their health, focus, and well-being by understanding the importance of sleep.

Why Focus on Sleep?

Sleep is not just rest — it’s essential for mental clarity, learning, and physical health. This project spreads awareness about how students can improve their sleep habits.

Better Focus

Students who sleep well can concentrate better and perform strongly in academics.

Emotional Balance

Quality sleep reduces stress, anxiety, and helps manage mood swings.

Physical Health

Good sleep strengthens the immune system and boosts energy for daily life.
